Welcome

Jason Gruetzmacher is the owner of 8BIT1337.com, a website created with the intention of participating in interesting discussions about the things that interest him.

Analytics

I have been interested in statistics and analytics ever since I got 'MicroLeague Baseball' for my Atari 400 back in the early 80s.

Back then I would purchase each years 'Sporting News' baseball guide and create the previous years World Series winners and Blue Jays teams and simulate tournaments.

These days I also play with Out of the Park Baseball as well as regularly read websites like FiveThirtyEight.com and FanGraphs.com

When I come across something that I find interesting in the world of analytics I'll discuss it here.

Atari XL/XE

My very first computer was an Atari 400, where I learned how to write basic programs and began using a computer regularly.

In this section I'll discuss the tools I use and the programs I've written using modern computers and using the great work done by a small but vibrant community.

Cord Cutting

I cut ties with cable back in 2014 and haven't looked back. Here I'll discuss how I've made that work, what I do and don't like about it, and anything interesting I stumble upon in the news.
